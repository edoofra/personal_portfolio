What I am focused on right now.

Current Focus #

I am currently focused on becoming stronger at the intersection of backend/platform engineering, product ownership, and AI-assisted delivery.

End-to-end ownership

Leading features from product discovery and technical analysis through launch, integration, and production follow-up.

AI agents in real workflows

Using Claude subagents, LLM workflows, and local AI experiments to reduce repetitive engineering work in practical, reviewable ways.

Modern Java backends

Deepening work around Java 21/25, Virtual Threads, Spring Boot, high-throughput I/O, and maintainable service design.

Reliability under load

Improving how distributed systems behave under peak traffic, partial failure, bursty processing, and time-sensitive operations.

Writing Direction #

I want this site to become useful through practical notes on:

  • backend engineering and service design;
  • Java, Spring Boot, and production patterns;
  • AWS event-driven systems;
  • AI-assisted development with LLMs, agents, and local models;
  • fintech reliability lessons learned from real delivery pressure.

What I Am Optimizing For #

The market is moving fast, especially around AI, but I think the strongest profile is still a grounded engineer who can ship, lead, and reason clearly. My focus is to combine reliable backend engineering with modern AI leverage in a way that produces measurable engineering value.
