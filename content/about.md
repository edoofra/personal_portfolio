I am a Software Engineer III focused on distributed systems, fintech platforms, and practical developer tooling. I care about systems that are easy to reason about when traffic is high, data is messy, and failures need to stay contained.

At Satispay I work on Welfare and Flexben products, where the engineering problems sit close to real business value: payment flows, reconciliation, order processing, reliability, and product iteration. I like that mix. It keeps the work technical, but never abstract.

## Interests

Outside the strict boundaries of delivery work, I like exploring the edges where infrastructure, product thinking, and AI-assisted development meet. I am especially interested in:

- Distributed architectures that stay understandable as they scale.
- Performance work that simplifies systems instead of making them clever for its own sake.
- Developer tools, agents, and automation that remove repetitive work while keeping humans responsible for design quality.
- Computer vision and machine learning, especially where prototypes can become useful product features.
- Writing and sharing what I learn through technical posts and small experiments.

I am fluent in Italian and English and based in Milan, Italy.
