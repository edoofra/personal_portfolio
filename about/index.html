About

I am a Software Engineer III focused on distributed systems, fintech platforms, and practical developer tooling. I care about systems that are easy to reason about when traffic is high, data is messy, and failures need to stay contained.

At Satispay I work on Welfare and Flexben products, where the engineering problems sit close to real business value: payment flows, reconciliation, order processing, reliability, and product iteration. I like that mix. It keeps the work technical, but never abstract.

Engineering Focus #

Backend platforms

Java, Spring Boot, RESTful microservices, MyBatis, JPA, Jersey, and production service design.

Distributed systems

Event-driven processing, fan-out workflows, high availability, circuit breakers, and fault isolation.

Cloud and data

AWS Lambda, EC2, ECS, S3, Kinesis, SQS, SNS, PostgreSQL, MySQL, Redshift, and Docker.

AI and automation

LLMs, Claude subagents, generative AI workflows, Python, YOLO, TensorFlow, and PyTorch.
